The ideas view celebrates what traditions share. This is the inverse: what each voice says that the others here don't — its idea clusters ranked by how far the tradition's share runs above its share of the whole corpus. Click an idea to see the passages behind it.
Distinctiveness is relative to this corpus: "distinctly Hindu" means "rare in the fourteen other text selections here," not "absent from all other human thought." A passage is an island when all ten of its nearest neighbours come from its own tradition. Clusters and their one-line names are AI-derived (k-means + Opus). An interpretive curiosity, not a theological claim.
